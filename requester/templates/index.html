What We Do

Inspire creativity in the world by providing users with novel and unique, never-seen-before stickers.

Inspiration

The vital role stickers and emojis play in allowing people to exress their thoughts and experssions digitally.

Training Model

A dual generator-discriminator network who learns over time with the correct training.

Mathematical Theory

Goal is to maximize the cost function of the discriminator using low-cross entropy and high-cross entropy.

Training Process

Discriminator learns which generated images are closest to real-world images over time, improving its output.
